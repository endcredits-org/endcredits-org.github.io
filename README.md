# End Credits

An art project by [Maciej Delmanowski](https://github.com/drybjed) that generates
cinematic-style end credits memorializing transgender people killed for being
transgender. Project website: [https://endcredits.org/](https://endcredits.org/)

Each page load randomly assembles a unique set of end credits — mixing real names
of trans people lost to violence, trans character names from television and film,
below-the-line film crew roles, LGBTQ+ music credits, and cinematic disclaimers —
scrolling against a black background.

## Purpose

Transgender people are killed at disproportionate rates worldwide, often with
little media coverage or public remembrance. This project reclaims the familiar
format of film end credits — typically a roll of names nobody reads — and fills
it with names that deserve to be seen, remembered, and honored.

## Usage

Open `index.html` in any modern web browser. The page will automatically
generate fresh end credits and reload itself every 15–60 seconds.

For memorial or awareness purposes, consider displaying the page on a screen
in a significant space — such as a community center, gallery, library,
LGBTQ+ venue, or vigil — where the scrolling names can serve as a quiet,
continuous reminder of lives lost.

## Data Sources

- **Memorial names** — [List of people killed for being transgender](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_people_killed_for_being_transgender) (Wikipedia)
- **Trans character names** — [List of transgender characters in television](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_transgender_characters_in_television) (Wikipedia) and [List of feature films with transgender characters](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_feature_films_with_transgender_characters) (Wikipedia)
- **Trans-significant cities** — Wikipedia articles for Compton's Cafeteria riot, Stonewall riots, Cooper's Donuts riot, Hirschfeld's Institute, Christine Jorgensen, Argentina's Gender Identity Law, and others
- **Trans-themed films** — A curated collection of films featuring transgender stories and characters
- **Music credits** — Curated songs from transgender and LGBTQ+ artists (Against Me!, ANOHNI, SOPHIE, Kim Petras, and others)
